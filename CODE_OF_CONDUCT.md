# Code of Conduct

[English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [简体中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md)

WeaveRT is committed to a friendly, safe, and constructive community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps build a positive environment:

- being respectful and patient
- giving and receiving feedback constructively
- focusing on what is best for the project and community
- showing empathy toward other contributors and users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- publishing others' private information without permission
- sustained disruptive behavior in issues, reviews, discussions, or other project spaces

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through a private channel when possible.
If the report also involves a security issue, follow `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this code of conduct.
Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent restrictions from project spaces.
